I.WHAT HIREFIRST IS FOR RELIANCE · HR Tech001 / 008
01 · What HireFirst is for Reliance

Problem · Solution · Impact.

Problem

Fragmented hiring stack.

  • · Multiple ATS systems · siloed candidate data across BUs
  • · Manual screening · recruiter day spent on data entry
  • · Inconsistent vendor SLAs · no central scoring
  • · No real-time hiring intelligence at the apex level
Solution

HireFirst · AI-Native Hiring OS.

  • · Unified pipeline from job creation to offer
  • · AI-assisted screening, interviewing, and decision support
  • · Centralised vendor governance and SLA tracking
  • · SAP OM as the source of truth · daily RFC sync
Impact for RIL

Recruiter day shifts to judgement.

  • · Vacancy → job in one click from SAP OM
  • · Standardised AI scoring with structured feedback loop
  • · Vendor performance becomes measurable
  • · Audit trail intact end-to-end · compliance bar held
II.LIVE FOR RIL TODAY · 9 MODULES SHIPPED TO SIT · HR Tech002 / 008
02 · Live for RIL today · 9 modules shipped to SIT

What HireFirst does today.

01Live · SIT

SAP OM · RFC Daily Sync

HireFirst connected to RIL's SAP OM via RFC. Daily automated sync pulls jobs, requisitions, org units, people, positions, vacancies, users, candidates, resumes, analytics. Plus one-click vacancy → job creation from SAP. Shipped 07 - Apr - 2026. Library-side issues for full OM sync in progress.

02Live · SIT

Negative Feedback Loop · AI Score Calibration

Recruiter downvotes a candidate's AI score with a structured reason. HireFirst recalibrates scoring for future candidates on the same job. Feedback appended to Scoring Notes · AI reads on every analysis. -5 ranking penalty per downvote.

03Live · SIT

Customisable Email Templates

Three ready-to-use templates · Interview Schedule (Online), Interview Schedule (Offline / In-person), Rejection. Rich text editor + dynamic variable substitution (name, role, date, link). Auto-trigger at hiring stage. Preview before save.

04Live · SIT

Candidate Portal

Candidate-facing surface for application, resume upload, status tracking. Will live at careersfirst.ril.com · server-side rendered so job-aggregator sites can scrape it cleanly.

05Live · SIT

Auto Enrich · Apollo.io

One click fills missing profile details from Apollo's 275M+ contact database · phone, email, role, company. Candidates section → open profile → Auto Enrich. Shipped 02 - Apr - 2026.

06Live · SIT

Instant Interview · Quick Job Creation

Create a job role on the fly directly inside the Instant Interview flow. No need to pre-set up a job. Resume upload + form filling auto-creates the job and shortlists in one pass.

07Live · SIT

Candidate Blocklist

Block candidates from being assigned to any job platform-wide while keeping the profile searchable for reference. Settings → Block List. Role-based access enforced (Leadership read-only). Shipped 03 - Apr - 2026.

08Live · Prod-grade

GCP Infra · Non-Prod + Prod

Project creation · billing · network with IDC connectivity · Cloud Run · Bastion Host · Postgres SQL · Artifact Registry · application deployed · SAP connectivity verified. Application reachability test to SAP system passed.

09Live · Prod

RIL Branding · "HireFirst"

Codename "Orion" replaced with "HireFirst" across the RIL-facing portal · logo, page title, all visible entry points. Fynd-internal instance keeps the Orion codename. Shipped 02 - Apr - 2026.

III.THE HIREFIRST ARCHITECTURE · 4 LAYERS · HR Tech003 / 008
03 · The HireFirst architecture · 4 layers

Four layers. One core.

HireFirst is built on a 4-layer platform. Most enterprise capabilities are pre-built; what's enabled on day one is a subset, expanded over the roadmap.

Layer 01

Enterprise Layer

Workforce planning · interview performance · background-verification APIs · vendor performance insights · budget & offer governance · board KPI dashboard · cost per hire · job requisition engine.

Layer 02

BU · Hiring Ops + AI / Interview

Approval workflow automation · role & skill template engine · interview panel mgmt · internal mobility · vendor scorecards · offer mgmt · digital offer & onboarding · campus & bulk hiring · AI video interview engine · proctoring · candidate experience portal · diversity & compliance.

Layer 03

Intelligence Core

Skill ontology graph · candidate knowledge graph · org ontology graph · AI matching engine · vendor question generator · bias detection · compensation benchmark · predictive hiring ML models · applied AI ontology.

Layer 04

Integration & Data

HRMS · finance systems · job portals · payroll · SSO / identity · data lake & warehouse. RIL deployment integrates SAP OM via RFC daily sync.

Five modules organise HireFirst's AI surface. Each card lists sub-features with explicit status. Live ships on SIT today. Roadmap is on the platform plan, not yet enabled.

Module 01

Job Intelligence

Precision from the start. Natural-language prompt → enterprise-grade JD with skills, OKRs, target companies, comp bench.

Candidate Intelligence

Data-driven talent discovery. Embeddings + vector search + fit-score & skill-gap analysis layered over a unified candidate database.

  • LiveProfile Enrichment · Apollo.io 275M+ contact database

  • RoadmapSemantic / RAG search · intent + context, not keywords
  • RoadmapAutomated dedup across multiple sources

AI assistant for the interviewer. Real-time transcription, follow-up suggestions, structured assessment, bias monitoring.

Optimising external talent partners. Assign job → submission → shortlist → interview → offer → join, with SLA + ranking layered over.

AI Decision Council

Multi-model orchestration. Strategic personas (Chairman, HR, Finance, Risk Analyser) debate a shortlist and produce a consolidated recommendation with objective scoring and an executive export.
