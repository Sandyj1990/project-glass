05 · Urban Ladder · live RR · Boltic as event collector

Urban Ladder · Boltic as event collector.

Live · RR · Urban Ladder

Reliance Retail's home-and-interiors brand Urban Ladder runs Boltic as the central event-collection and routing layer across a heterogeneous stack: storefront, order management, Kapture (interiors CRM), CleverTap (engagement), GA4 (analytics), Sentry (monitoring). Events ingested by Boltic, fanned out reliably in parallel to downstream analytics and engagement platforms.

Challenge

Heterogeneous stack, no event spine.

  • · Collect events from multiple sources and fan them out to the right analytics and engagement platforms
  • · Stitch cross-system workflows so customer-facing communications and internal alerts fire reliably, without engineering effort for every new flow
  • · Alert mechanism to notify engineering on errors logged in Sentry during development
Primary use case

Boltic as the event collector.

Boltic deployed as Urban Ladder's central event-collection and routing layer. Events generated across the platform are ingested by Boltic and reliably forwarded to multiple downstream analytics and engagement platforms in parallel.

Frontend events flow into Boltic, then land in the data lake or get integrated with engagement tools to drive conversion. Two broad outcomes:

  • · Improve customer communication and lead generation · CRM lift
  • · Act as an orchestration layer between systems · generate workflows
