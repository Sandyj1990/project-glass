01 · What ALP is for Reliance

From scouting to keys-in-hand · in one system.

Problem

NSO ran on email + spreadsheets.

  • · Document exchange ran over email · ALP replaces with in-app uploads + comments per lead (PRD §Lead Creation)
  • · Approvals tracked manually across RE, BT, NSO, Format CFO, Apex
  • · Site assessment notes scattered · no audit trail
  • · No single state-by-state or format-by-format AOP funnel
What ALP does

One record per candidate site. End-to-end.

  • · Lead creation form + auto-generated Lead ID (ALP-2026-NNNN)
  • · 7-tab Lead Details · Overview → Site Visit → Validation → Approvals → Legal → Fit-Out → Payments
  • · 11-state lifecycle · ageing in days from BT-submission
  • · Role-Based Access Control scoped to state (RE) and format-category (BT)
  • · Activity log + backend audit trail per lead
Impact today

GJ-cluster pilot live, Apr-2026.

  • · Live data captured for the GJ-cluster pilot · including TEST leads from UAT
  • · 11-status taxonomy live · auto-applied filters by role
  • · SAML SSO with Reliance accounts · IRM-compliant
  • · 4 manual-update tabs ready for Phase 2 integration with Board / Sirion / R-Dash / SAP

03 · Lead lifecycle · how the platform works

11 statuses · 7 tabs · 7 timeline stages · live screens.

Every lead in ALP carries an ID, sits in one of 11 statuses, and progresses through a 7-tab Details page. The Progress Timeline widget at the top of every Lead Details collapses the journey into 7 stages from Site Details through Rent Declaration.
