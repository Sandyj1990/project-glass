Pillars · Plan · Buy · Make/Move · Sell

Embedded in every F&L stage · Plan · Buy · Make · Sell.

Plan

Demand → assortment → space.

Assortment plan · store-led assortment · trends research · cohorting · planning agent (Cortex) · audience building. Online storefront feeds back signals.

Buy

Trend → design → vendor.

3D Design & Collaboration · PLM · Vendor management · Cost negotiation & PO · Costing engine · Trend-to-Design agent.

Make / Move

Factory → DC → shelf.

Manufacturing management · Vendor scan-and-pack · Store-to-shelf automation · Route optimisation · Load and network planning · Transport planning.

Sell

Discover → buy → serve.

Catalog & pricing · Search & discovery · Payments & checkout · Order management & returns · Customer care · Customer service · Auto stock replenishment · Margin optimisation · Reconciliation automation.
