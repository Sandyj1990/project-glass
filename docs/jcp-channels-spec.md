# JCP Channel Coverage Gallery

> Source of truth for `/jcp/channels/` — a visual gallery of the storefronts and mobile apps that the JioCommerce Platform powers.
>
> · Channel directory mirrors `docs/jcp-notes-compilation/Channel_Coverage_JCP.pdf` (Feb 21, 2026) — 78 channels across 10 clusters.
> · Storefront screenshots come from RBL OS Synthetic Monitor (`https://rbl-os.vercel.app/synthetic`, last sync 5 Apr 2026).
> · Mobile-app icons + first phone screenshot come from Google Play Store, scraped via `tools/build_jcp_channels.py`.
> · Channels with no public consumer surface (B2B, in-store/OMS only, non-commerce sites, planned) are listed for completeness with a one-line reason.
