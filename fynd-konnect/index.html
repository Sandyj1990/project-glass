PILLAR 1 · JIOMART 3P SELLER PLATFORM (FYND KONNECT) · Integration Backbone004 / 007
04 · Pillar 1 · JioMart 3P Seller Platform (Fynd Konnect)

The seller-side rail for JioMart.

The JioMart Channel is the largest channel Fynd Konnect has built and operates on top of Fynd Commerce. It is the bridge between JioMart's three core systems and the seller's choice of OMS, WMS or ERP, and is directly responsible for JioMart's 3P seller scale-up.

Core JioMart systems integratedInventory · Catalogue & orders · Storefront. Three core systems unified behind a single seller experience on Fynd Commerce

How the integration works.

Fynd Konnect abstracts JioMart's three core systems behind a single seller experience on Fynd Commerce. The integration contract is unified at the platform level so that every seller (regardless of which OMS, WMS or ERP they run) connects through one rail.

Inventory

Flows from the seller's source-of-truth (Fynd OMS, third-party OMS / WMS / ERP, or aggregator) through Konnect into JioMart's inventory system in real time.

Catalogue

Catalogue and vertical mapping handled through JioMart's catalogue system.

Orders

Created on Fynd Konnect from JioMart's storefront and routed to the seller's fulfilment system.

Shipment status

Flows back to JioMart for LSP coordination and customer communications.

Onboarding

Sellers onboard once on Fynd Commerce, complete location and SKU mapping, and become immediately transactable on JioMart, without bespoke per-brand or per-aggregator integration work.

The aggregator ecosystem.

RTI and hyperlocal sellers reach JioMart through key third-party platforms that Konnect normalises into a single integration contract. This eliminates per-seller builds and gives JioMart instant access to every brand already running on these platforms. 7 platforms · one integration contract.

Enabling international expansion
Live

Taking a brand into a new market.

How Fynd Konnect compresses time-to-market for international expansion.

01

Catalogue mapping

The brand's catalogue (already on Fynd Commerce) is mapped to the target marketplace's category tree using Konnect's marketplace-specific mappers.

02

Pricing & inventory rules

Market-specific pricing (with currency, tax and duty handling) and inventory allocation rules are configured.

03

Order routing

Orders from the international marketplace flow back into Fynd OMS, routing to the appropriate fulfilment node (cross-border or in-market).

04

Returns & post-purchase

Returns and reverse logistics handled through the marketplace's flow, with SAP postings handled through the same Konnect posting layer.

Net effect. A new market is opened without a bespoke integration project. The same RBL brand operating in India can be live on Noon, Lazada or Shopee within weeks, not quarters. Outcome: International expansion becomes a configuration exercise rather than an engineering project.
