## Reconciliation note · 105 vs 78

The original deck headline number of "105 B2C" channels includes ~39 RBL retail-brand microsites (Hamleys, Sephora, Coach, etc.) that share clusters and don't have a dedicated tracker row. The Migration Tracker enumerates 66 distinct channels; +12 RBL/JCP cluster shares = 78 total in the Migration Tracker view. Both are correct, just two different denominators.

This register uses **78 total · 68 Live** as canonical (the Migration Tracker view) per `/numbers`.
