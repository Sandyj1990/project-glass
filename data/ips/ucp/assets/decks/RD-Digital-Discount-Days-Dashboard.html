§6 · Caveats & open questions
  • Attribution vs. overlapThese numbers show transactors who were also messaged — not causal proof. To attribute revenue, campaign send dates, first-transaction dates, and a pre/post or holdout comparison are needed.
  • Date scopeAnalysis restricted to Apr 11–19 (9 days). April 20th data (1,466 line items, 488 transactors unique to that date) excluded to align with campaign measurement window.
  • Service-line exclusionsExcluded L2s: HA/TELECOM/CE/IT SPARES, CONCESSIONAIRE SERVICES, ENHANCEMENT, SERVICES. SWAPEASY & CASHIFY kept as real product sales.
  • 5,693 vs. 5,66033 transactors appear in the sales file on service-only tickets — they technically transacted but bought no product line.
