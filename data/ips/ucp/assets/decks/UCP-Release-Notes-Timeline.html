Campaign Overview

10 RCS campaigns executed between 11–19 April 2026, targeting dormant customers and JioMart electronics buyers across regions (AP/TG and Rest of India) on both Android and iOS. This was UCP's first large-scale conversion-tracked campaign for Reliance Digital.

Engagement Clusters

Two distinct patterns emerged: High-open campaigns (~32–44% open rate) on Android showed strong visibility, while low-open campaigns (~7–11%) on iOS had significantly higher click-through-on-read rates (CTOR 9–15%) — indicating stronger purchase intent when the message was actually read.

Transactor Segment Lift

5,693 campaign recipients transacted at Reliance Digital stores during the 9-day window. These customers showed dramatically higher engagement vs. the overall audience:

Top Brands

Samsung (₹3.98 Cr) led the pack, followed by Apple (₹3.19 Cr) and LG (₹2.59 Cr). Top SKUs included Voltas & LG split ACs (seasonal demand) and Apple iPhone 17 Pro Max.

Geography

South zone dominated at 44.5% of revenue (₹9.07 Cr), followed by West (20.3%), East (19.4%), and North (15.9%). Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Tamil Nadu were the top three contributing states.

Key Takeaway

This was UCP's first large-scale conversion-tracked campaign for Reliance Digital — establishing a replicable playbook for electronics retail campaigns. From audience segmentation through RCS delivery to offline POS conversion tracking, the entire workflow was powered end-to-end by UCP.

Saturday 19 April alone generated ₹4.08 Cr (20% of total revenue in one day), highlighting strong weekend purchase behaviour that future campaigns can optimise around.
