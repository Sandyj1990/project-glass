## Release · Coupon code embedding

UCP's Campaign Manager now supports embedded coupon codes · shared (one code for all) or personalised (unique per customer). Upload a CSV/ZIP of customer-to-coupon mappings, and UCP handles matching and injection at delivery time. No more duct-taping coupons across tools.

## What's coming next

Integration with JCP Coupons and Central Promotion Engine for real-time coupon fetching · no more pre-uploading files.
