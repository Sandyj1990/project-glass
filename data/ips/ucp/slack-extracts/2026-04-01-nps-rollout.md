## Release · NPS Survey SMS (powered by UCP) · 79 formats · ~7,000 stores

We've rolled out Net Promoter Score (NPS) surveys via SMS across 79 formats of Reliance Retail and RBL formats (~7,000 stores).

## How it works

Every in-store purchase on POS automatically triggers an SMS within 1-2 hours · customers tap a link, rate their experience (0-10), and share feedback. That simple.

## Why this matters

NPS will help us better understand customer satisfaction at the store level, identify areas for improvement, and drive more customer-centric decisions across businesses.

## What's coming next

- Two additional survey questions rolling out soon based on central retail team's request
- Analytics dashboards in progress · stay tuned
