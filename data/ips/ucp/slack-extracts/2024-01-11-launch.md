## Released · Reliance Retail IDentity

As part of the whole initiative to unify customers across Reliance Retail, we onboarded all of the **310 million customers** across all different offline and online channels, platforms and formats in Reliance Retail. Every user now has a singular identity across all of the said channels, platforms and formats.

This Identity will help us create, what would eventually mature into a single living system that consistently re-engages a user by using their various activities across different BUs, verticals, channels, platforms · and grow the business.

## Public Channel Connectivity

After consistent back-and-forth with Jio InfoSec team, we now also have a public connectivity path unlocked with UCP, vetted by InfoSec. RBL brand owners and external Fynd-built brands · let's connect.

## Jio InfoSec Alignment

With a lot of meetings and scrutiny from InfoSec team, especially due to the nature of data we collect being PII, we have not only gotten our platform completely vetted by them but also aligned ourselves to their standards.

## Sanctions Screening Service v2.0

Cross-references the Sanctioned users list of approximately 25 lakh suspicious names and organisations along with their correlated addresses, returns the same to JioMart which utilises this to disable "Pay with UPI via WhatsApp Pay" checkout option. Now enhanced to include Global Sanctioned Users as provided by Dow Jones.
