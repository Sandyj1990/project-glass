## New releases

### Reliance Retail Authentication

As part of the initiative to unify customers across Reliance Retail, we had onboarded 310 million customers across offline and online businesses. Starting today we are powering the OTP system for **JioMart**, with **AJIO** to follow suit soon. This is a key milestone towards establishing the Reliance Retail Account Centre · a unified account and profile hub for all Reliance Retail storefronts.

### Address Optimisation (powered by ACL team)

Address Optimisation is a service powered by Address Engine and built by the ACL team. We have integrated this service with UCP to optimise addresses in JioMart to reduce RTO and delivery failure cases. Running pilot on existing addresses of 76 pincodes · already **optimised 69,318 addresses and counting**.

### New channel integrations

Having integrated with JioMart, Netmeds, AJIO and Tira in past · we are now live with **MilkBasket** (2 million users).
