## 1. Auto Enrich · Apollo.io Integration

With Auto Enrich, recruiters can now fill in missing profile details with a single click · powered by Apollo's 275M+ contact database.

Available now: Candidates section → open any profile → Auto Enrich

## 2. Instant Interview · Quick Job Creation

You can now create a new job role on the fly directly inside the Instant Interview flow · no need to set up a job in advance.

Available now: Interviews → Instant Interview

## 3. Branding Update · RIL Portal

The "Orion" name and logo have been replaced with "HireFirst" across the RIL-facing portal. This applies to the logo, page title, and all visible entry points for the RIL team. The Fynd-internal instance is not affected.
