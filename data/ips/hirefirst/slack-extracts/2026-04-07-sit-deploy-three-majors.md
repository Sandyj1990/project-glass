## 1. Negative Feedback Loop · AI Score Calibration

Recruiters can now downvote a candidate's AI score with a structured reason. HireFirst uses that feedback to recalibrate scoring for future candidates on the same job.

1. Jobs → Candidate Pool → click 👎 → pick a reason → add a comment → submit
2. Feedback appends to the job's Scoring Notes — AI reads this on every future analysis
3. Downvoted candidates receive a soft ranking penalty (-5 per downvote)
4. Scoring Notes visible and editable on JD page and Candidates page
5. Customise reason labels: Settings → Score Feedback

## 2. Customisable Email Templates

Three ready-to-use templates now available — editable via rich text editor, with dynamic variable substitution (name, role, date, link, etc.).

1. Interview Schedule — Online
2. Interview Schedule — Offline / In-person
3. Rejection Email

Templates auto-trigger at the relevant hiring stage. Preview before saving.
Settings → Email Templates

## 3. SAP OM Integration · RFC Daily Sync

HireFirst is now connected to Reliance's SAP OM via RFC integration. Daily automated sync pulls the latest jobs, requisitions, and org data with no manual intervention.

1. Synced: Org Units · People · Jobs + Requisitions · Positions · Vacancies · Users · Candidates + Resumes · Analytics
2. Plus one-click vacancy → job creation directly from SAP
