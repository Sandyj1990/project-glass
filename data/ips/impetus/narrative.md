# Impetus

> Reliance's AI-first platform for Fashion and Lifestyle retail.


## What it does

Impetus is the AI platform powering Plan, Buy/Make, Move, Sell across Reliance Fashion and Lifestyle. 14 IPs in production today. Took a 9-month design-to-shelf cycle and rebuilt it as a 3.6-day vendor-to-store loop.

Trend research, 3D design, manufacturing, store delivery, in-store sell, customer listening, and real-time decisioning · all running on one stack with daily prod deploys.

## Why this matters for Reliance

Fashion is Reliance's most complex retail vertical. Impetus standardised the AI brain across every brand · Trends, Azorte, Yousta, Fashion Factory, AJIO, RBL · so a Trend-to-Design improvement at AJIO automatically helps Trends, and a Cortex planning win at Trends helps Azorte.
