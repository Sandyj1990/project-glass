03 · How it works · 5 steps from counter to cash

One spine. Diagnostic → quote → POS → Liquidation Partner.

The same flow used for every mobile exchange today is the flow every category (laptops, TVs, home & living) will reuse as the platform expands (§06 roadmap).

1
Counter sign-in

Customer enters in-store; SwapEasy auto-binds the My Jio Store address. Promo banner offers up to ₹8,000 bonus on the day's bump-up SKU.

2
Pick brand and model

8 OEMs visible by default (Vivo, Tecno, Samsung, Realme, POCO, OPPO, Nothing, Nokia); search-for-models field for any other brand.

3
Get a quote

Standardised diagnostic returns a fair-market quote. Example: iPhone 17 Pro Max (12 GB / 512 GB) → ₹1,05,500. Get Exact Value runs the full diagnostic; otherwise the quoted ceiling stands.

4
POS credit applied

Approved exchange value is applied as credit at the SAP point-of-sale terminal; new device walks out with the customer the same visit. SAP captures the uplift as incremental revenue (the ₹3.23 Cr Feb '26 figure is the sum of these SAP entries).

5
Liquidation Partner routing & reconcile

Old device is routed to the highest-bidding liquidation partner active in that zone, with a 3-day pickup SLA, full ledger entry, and audit trail. The operator surface for this loop ships in §04 below.

06 · 6-month roadmap + vision

Two axes. Same spine.

Deeper categories within electronics, then beyond electronics into broader lifestyle goods. Each expansion reuses the same diagnostic-quote-Liquidation Partner-POS spine that is live for mobiles today.

Phase 1 · Months 1–4
Roadmap

Beyond Mobiles, Within Electronics

  • Laptops & tablets. Highest near-term revenue lever after mobiles. Component-level diagnostics (battery health, screen, keyboard, ports) and zone-specific grading. Refurbisher Liquidation Partners onboarded with category capability.
  • Smartwatches & wearables. Lower-ticket but high-volume. Bundle exchange offers with new device sales.
  • TVs & large appliances. Reuse the Delivery-Partner doorstep QC flow already designed for online exchanges. Integrate with the existing Reliance Digital home-delivery network.
Phase 2 · Months 4–6+
Roadmap

Beyond Electronics

  • Home & living. Furniture, décor and kitchenware exchange. Buy-back partners differ (re-furnishers, charities, secondary marketplaces); the quote-grade-orchestrate spine holds.
  • Kitchenware & small appliances. High-attach categories at JioMart, Reliance Smart and Trends Home. Opens a re-commerce flywheel across formats.
  • Online + omnichannel exchange. Doorstep diagnostics and re-quote logic to bring exchange to AJIO, JioMart and Reliance Digital online journeys. Completes the omnichannel loop.

Vision · re-commerce OS for Reliance

The re-commerce spine for Reliance. Wherever exchange applies.

SwapEasy is the re-commerce operating system underneath every Reliance retail format. Wherever a customer has something old to trade, and wherever a partner has the appetite to buy it back.

At scale: any customer, in any Reliance store or app, across any category, can scan, get a fair quote, see brand-funded top-ups, and walk out with a new product. With the old item routed to the highest-bidding partner in their zone, fully reconciled and audited.

Strategic value to the group
  • ~5% format-revenue uplift at maturity
  • 8–10% margin on resale vs ~5% on new
  • Richer first-party data on upgrade cycles, brand affinity and price sensitivity
  • Defensible moat as India's organised re-commerce category formalises
  • B2B platform asset JCP can extend to non-Reliance retailers in time
