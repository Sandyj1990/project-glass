02 · What it does for you

Revolutionising apparel manufacturing. IntelliVerse IS the Manufacturing Execution System (MES) — a unified AI-native vendor OS that connects every stage of the factory.

- The challenges (before): scattered tools & manual reports · WhatsApp / email coordination · data duplication & loss. - Key benefits: Unified Vendor OS · Smarter Pre-Production · Seamless Daily Reporting · Proactive in Quality · Faster to Packing · End-to-End Traceability. - System modules: Pre-Production Order Summary · Daily Production Reporting (DPR) · Quality Audit Module · Full QC Visibility · IntelliPack Integration · Capacity Declaration · Integrations & Webhooks · AI Orchestration.

This is the backbone of how goods move from suppliers to stores. Suppliers use it to scan, pack and ship products and can access the mobile shipping app by scanning a QR code (no URLs to remember). Quality inspectors check goods at the factory and now catch problems at 10% completion instead of waiting until 70%, saving huge re-manufacturing costs. Sourcing teams manage purchase orders, new partners can be added through settings instead of needing custom engineering work. Operations teams get real-time system health monitoring, problems are diagnosed in minutes, not hours.

04 · What improved

Quality inspectors catch defects at 10% order completion (vs 70%), saving 2-3 weeks rework. Suppliers see one consolidated timeline per product per month (vs 3 separate timelines). Format heads can delay delivery dates and the system recalculates penalties automatically. New partners (AJIO, LivSmart, Fynd Create) added via configuration, not custom code. PO-to-Shipping cycle: 55 → 30 days (45% faster).

Expected metric impactcatching defects at 10% instead of 70% saves the cost of re-manufacturing 60% more of an order. The 55 → 30 day PO-to-ship compression is the headline supply chain win of the quarter — every additional day of pipeline cycle ties up working capital and pushes back markdowns.
