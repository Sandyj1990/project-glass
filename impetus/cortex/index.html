02 · What it does for you

A unified command center to monitor, plan, and act across your entire retail ecosystem from a single pane — with real-time visibility into stores, inventory, workforce, and customer metrics.

Open one screen and the whole business is there — revenue, gross margin %, store performance, inventory health, vendor compliance and Annual Plan tracking, refreshed daily with no reports to request. The system surfaces what matters: aged inventory piling up, stores running out of stock, vendors missing delivery targets. Built-in AI explains why a number moved, not just that it moved. Works on Desktop, iPad and Mobile (iOS), use it in meetings and store visits.

Six modules live: Portfolio Health, Margin Analysis, Store Intelligence, Inventory Health, Vendor Performance and AOP (Annual Operating Plan) Tracking.

Operating cadence
Cortex IS the cadence layer
All decisions and operations are governed by a six-cadence Retail Clock — real-time (milliseconds · RFID, POS, IoT) → minutes (low-stock alerts, footfall) → hourly (store vs plan) → shift (manager scorecards) → daily (sales, aged stock) → monthly (model retraining). With AI, every cadence compresses; monthly reviews become continuous. Cortex is where this clock lives.
03 · How Many People Use It · Features Live Today
Sub-section · Command Center [1/2] + [2/2]

Command Center · the unified monitor-plan-act surface

Monitor, plan, and act across your entire retail ecosystem from a single pane — real-time visibility into stores, inventory, workforce and customer metrics.

Six modules live: Store Operations (real-time health, footfall, conversion, staffing per store) · Inventory Intelligence (stock-level visibility, auto-replenishment triggers, demand forecasting) · Workforce Management (scheduling, attendance, performance dashboards) · Campaign Control (launch, monitor, optimise AI campaigns with unified analytics) · Customer 360° (NPS, loyalty, purchase history, engagement) · Alerts & Escalations (configurable thresholds with automated workflows).

Sub-section · AI Agents

AI Agents · Trend-to-Design

Transforms trend + brand context into manufacturable design options and auto-generates complete tech packs (specs, BOM, trims, artwork) for vendor-ready handoff.

Sibling agents in the same family: Planning Agent (Cortex) — builds a store-realistic Plan of Record and converts it into fixture/slot-level VM layouts, with exception-led replans. Agentic Marketing — autonomously builds and optimises campaigns (audiences, creatives, timing, measurement) using customer signals. Ask Impetus · AI Business Analyst — turns cross-system retail data into executive-ready answers, explains "why," quantifies impact, and recommends the next best fix.

Sub-section · Agentic Control Tower

Agentic Control Tower

An agent-driven control tower that detects, prioritises, and resolves business exceptions in real time. This is where the Retail Clock cadences meet automatic action — every alert is an exception flowing into a workflow that the system attempts to resolve before it reaches a human.

Sub-section · Store Intelligence

Store Intelligence

AI-powered store intelligence that decodes demand, competition, and localised growth opportunities. Every store becomes a node in a sensing network — local demand signals, competitive density and demographics feed the assortment, pricing and marketing decisions for that catchment.

04 · What improved

Leadership gets a daily portfolio view without asking for reports. You can find exactly where profit is leaking and drill down in 2 clicks. Underperforming zones and stores are sorted and surfaced instantly. Aged inventory (₹85.1 Cr >180 days) and stockouts (805 stores running out) are flagged automatically. Vendor ranking and lead times are live, with on-time delivery and fill rate tracked. You can compare actuals to the annual plan by category, channel or format. The Buy Plan module shortlists 3,492 styles flowing straight to the Costing Engine.

Expected metric impactdecisions move from monthly review meetings to the same screen everyone uses daily — with AI explaining the "why" behind every number. The shortest path between a number changing and someone deciding to act on it.
