Impetus PLM.

(Product Lifecycle Management)

02 · What it does for you

Impetus PLM replaces Centric PLM for design, tech pack and product development. Designers create and manage styles, tech packs and material lists (Bill of Materials) with clear high-quality images and simpler navigation. Technical designers generate detailed specs that flow automatically into costing and production systems. Buyers manage fabric libraries and vendor allocations.

Everything connects end-to-end, to Costing Engine for pricing, to EAN (product barcodes) and to NextWave/FTF for trend data. One login (SSO, Single Sign-On) via Impetus Platform — no separate credentials.

04 · What improved

Design documentation time reduced 80% via auto-generated tech specs. Full Centric PLM migration completed — single platform for all product data. One-login access (SSO) via Impetus Platform, no separate credentials needed. High-quality tech pack images with simplified, intuitive UI. Full style lifecycle: moodboard → design → tech pack → costing → production. Connected to CMT for consistent master data and to Costing Engine, barcodes and FTF. AI-generated walkthrough videos + open house sessions for rapid onboarding.

Expected metric impact80% less design documentation time means designers ship more styles per person per season. Replacing Centric eliminates a per-seat licence cost and the hand-off lag between Design and Costing. Format-by-format rollout (Yousta Mens → Yousta All → Azorte → Fashion Factory → Trends) means migration risk is bounded per format.
