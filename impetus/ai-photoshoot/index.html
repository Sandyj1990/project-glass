01 · What it does for you

AI Photoshoot turns a single garment image — flatlay, mannequin, model, or 3D render — into a complete on-model shoot in studio or lifestyle settings.

FLATLAY → AI MODEL
Turn basic flatlay product images into fully styled, on-model visuals in studio or lifestyle settings.
MANNEQUIN → AI MODEL
Transform mannequin shots into photorealistic images with AI-generated human models and backgrounds.
MODEL SWAP
Swap real model faces or poses with AI-generated alternatives for diversity, consistency or brand fit.
3D RENDER → AI MODEL
Convert 3D garment renders into photorealistic on-model images, bridging digital sampling and visual marketing.
AI MODEL VIDEOS
5 to 10-second product videos from AI images for richer PDPs and social content.
FACE CONSISTENCY
Keep the same AI model face across product collections for visual uniformity in catalogues and campaigns.
DYNAMIC ENSEMBLE
Combine multiple garments — top + pants — into styled full-look images with one consistent model.
FACE SWAP
Refresh existing catalogues by swapping model faces — preserve garment, lighting and expression. Avoids the cost of a fresh physical shoot.
Why this approach
SKU-specific AI model training, not generic generative
A model is trained uniquely per SKU so the AI learns fabric behaviour, garment fit, fall and texture — outputs stay SKU-faithful. No studio, location, photographer, model or editing required. AI-generated models across ethnicities, ages and body types. Catalogues processed at scale into on-model shoots. Brand-controlled styling for model type, studio or lifestyle background. Works on standard PDP images — no raw studio source needed.
02 · Why AI photoshoot · the problem with traditional

Five constraints brand partners hit on every season — most of them structural, not creative.

PROBLEM 01
High cost & complexity
Photographers, stylists, studios, models — expensive, slow, resource-heavy.
PROBLEM 02
Geographic & cultural representation
Hard to source the right model diversity or location setup quickly for global reach.
PROBLEM 03
Time-to-market pressure
Fashion is seasonal; brand owners need quick turnaround on new styles and collections.
PROBLEM 04
Video is growing
Marketplaces increasingly use video for interactivity. Adding professional videography on top of photography compounds cost and timeline.
PROBLEM 05
Poor imagery cuts conversion
Many brands fall back to flat-lay or mannequin shots due to budget. Marketplace data shows lack of model imagery reduces conversion.
Traditional photoshoot · vs · AI Photoshoot
People involved

Photographer, models, stylists, makeup, lighting crew, editors.

Logistics

Studio booking, travel, equipment setup, large-team coordination.

Effort

Multiple revisions, scheduling conflicts, dependencies.

Output

Limited per shoot, longer turnaround.

AI Photoshoot

Send the shoot brief and garment images. The AI handles the rest — model generation, styling, background, lighting, post-production. Catalogue-scale output in hours rather than weeks.
